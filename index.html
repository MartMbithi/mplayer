Mplayer Features

Easy to Customize

You can customize order of songs in any user-playlist, click sort icon and click 'customize order yourself'

Multi-Select Functionality and Multiple Queues

Mplayer is shipped with a powerful multi select functionality and powerful multiple queues which makes it more powerful and one of the best music players.

100% Free

MPlayer is totally free, it doesnt require any internet connectivity in order to operate and no adds. Our users satisfaction and happiness is the only payment we receive.

Sleep timer

Using this feature, you can set the timer for music to stop. Dedicated to all the people who put their earphones in and go to sleep. This eventually saves your battery life.
